Settings

When would you like your next Waffle?

Midnight GMT Standard time 00:00:00
Midnight Local time 00:00:00

By default, everyone in the world gets a fresh Waffle served up at the same time (midnight GMT). Change this setting if you would like to have your Waffle arrive at midnight your local time. This might mean you have to wait a bit longer for your next one (e.g. USA) or perhaps you may enjoy it sooner (e.g. Australia).

This cannot be changed whilst you have a delicious Waffle in progress.
